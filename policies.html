Clinic Policies

Please read our clinic policies carefully before starting treatment with us. If you have any questions or concerns, our staff will be happy to assist you.

Infection Control

We believe that infection control is important to prevent cross contamination of diseases from patient to patient. Therefore:

  • All instruments are presoaked in disinfectant solution
  • Instruments are pouched before autoclaving
  • We use disposable items for single use
  • New polishing brushes are issued to patients

Appointments

  • Arrive 5-10 minutes before appointment time
  • Some waiting time may be expected
  • Walk-ins for emergencies only
  • New patients should register before appointments
  • 24-hour notice for cancellations
  • RM20 fee for late cancellations

Patient Rights

  • Right to be informed of medical condition
  • Right to dignity and respect
  • Right to request treatment reports
  • Right to ask questions about fees/treatment
